About Us

Our company places great emphasis on making products that meet changing market trends, customer (typically parent) expectations, and primarily the needs of children. We strive to ensure that the content of our books represents both the modern and authentic fairy-tale world because the rising generations must evolve in time, but they also need to know about the wonderful storytelling of their predecessors. These stories gave the young adult an aid and guidance for the independent life ahead of them. It was a moral guide that can still serve as a benchmark for learning and maintaining the correct values.

Childrens and Books

For the little ones, being unable to read yet, the same information reaches through the visual world, with an emphasis on expanding their knowledge primarily. Based on the capabilities of this age group, a significant part of our publications will be represented by the foam books that have already revolutionized the book market, especially the publications for the younger age group.
One of the most important features of books made of EVA foam is that they are light, easy to turn, and, thanks to the cardboard sheet covered with foil, liquids spilt on can be easily wiped off. They don’t have sharp edges, they can’t be easily torn apart, so they don’t cause injury.

About Our Books

The goods offered by us have been subjected to international official safety checks. The materials used in the process like the paper, offset mixture inks and the used hotmelt adhesive are all subject of testing.

The product’s specification had passed the UK and EU standard expectations as follows:
  • Standard consumer safety specification for toy safety
  • Physical and mechanical properties
  • Flammability
  • Heavy Element Content in Paint and Similar Surface-Coating Materials and Substrate Materials
